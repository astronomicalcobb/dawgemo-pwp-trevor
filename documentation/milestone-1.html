Milestone One

Purpose, Audience, and Goals

Purpose: This website is aimed to update Husky Well & Pump Service's current website and offer potential customers the opportunity to learn about our company, see our work, get needed information, and the ability to contact us.

Audience: The audience for this website will be potential customers, general contractors, job applicants, and other drilling businesses.

Goals: My goals for this website is to create something that my family and I can be proud of, inform people about our business, show potential customers we are continuously keeping up with the times, allow potential customers an easy and enjoyable way to get in contact with us, and to showcase the work we've done.

Persona

Name: Ryan Garcia

Age: 37

Profession: Self Employed General Contractor

Technology: Mac user that's a little behind the times. Ryan has an iPhone 14 (6.1-inch) and MacBook Pro (15-inch, 2016).

Attitudes and Behaviors: Ryan is a busy man who's always on the go, as he has many projects he oversees. While working, Ryan only uses his iPhone to search for subcontractors; he will spend a short amount of time per business to see if they are a good candidate. At home, he uses his laptop and spends more time researching good subcontractors.

Frustrations and Needs: When searching for subcontractors on the go, Ryan needs to find the about-info of a business quickly, so he can contact them right away. Ryan hates bad websites, and will not hesitate to look for another business that takes their webpage seriously. ESPECIALLY when that website doesn't have a dark-mode.

Goals: Ryan's job is to get information about a subcontractor quickly, so he can get a quote from them, they can make a deal, and the subcontractor can get to work. His goal is to determine if a business is the right fit for the job and to hire them by looking at, via their website, some work they've done, what areas they work in, and their contact information.

User Story: I need a water well drilled for a site I am overseeing that is going to have a home built on it.
